FAQs

What is Angor and how does it differ from traditional crowdfunding platforms?

Angor is a decentralized crowdfunding platform built on the Bitcoin network. Unlike traditional platforms, it employs trustless mechanisms, such as secure timelock contracts and controlled stage releases, ensuring transparency and mitigating risks for investors.

How do investors maintain control over their funds on Angor?

Investors lock their funds into time-locked contracts at various stages. This unique feature allows investors to actively control their contributions, with the flexibility to recover funds or accept penalties as needed.

What sets Angor apart in terms of security and fraud prevention?

Angor introduces penalties for early investors (seeders) during recovery, adding an extra layer of security. This, coupled with founder-controlled releases and transparent mechanisms, significantly mitigates the risk of fraud.

Can project founders misuse the funds on Angor?

No, Angor incorporates founder-controlled stage releases, utilizing time-locked contracts and founder keys. This structured approach ensures that funds are released at predefined intervals, reducing the risk of mismanagement.

How does Angor contribute to the Bitcoin network's growth?

Angor encourages Bitcoin holders to allocate funds for investment, unlocking resources that contribute to the Bitcoin network. This aligns with our vision of making investments work for users while strengthening the broader Bitcoin ecosystem.

Are there any limitations on distributing or sublicensing Angor?

Yes, Angor cannot be distributed or sublicensed without explicit permission from Softchains, the project behind Angor. This measure is in place to maintain the integrity of the platform and protect the interests of all stakeholders.
